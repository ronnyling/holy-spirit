Speaker: Value Analyst B

Stock Alpha Berhad looks cheap on price-to-book and price-to-earnings, but I am not convinced it is safe. Earnings declined sharply in the latest year. Free cash flow was weak. The high dividend yield may be caused by the share price dropping, not by business strength.

If the company keeps paying dividends while cash flow is falling, the dividend may be unsustainable. This could be a yield trap rather than a good income stock.
